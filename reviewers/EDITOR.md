## 5. The weak cues (40)

The preposition batch got the most formulaic cue drafting — accurate, but nobody
has ever wondered "how do you concede a minimum?". These are the opening card of
each carousel, so they carry real weight. Rewrite the ones that grate.

- **0201** A propósito — "How do you change the subject?"
- **0202** Por lo visto — "How do you report something you heard secondhand?"
- **0203** A decir verdad — "How do you preface an honest admission?"
- **0204** A la larga — "How do you talk about the long run?"
- **0205** A toda costa — "How do you insist it must happen no matter what?"
- **0206** En consecuencia — "How do you draw a consequence, formally?"
- **0207** Hace poco — "How do you say something happened recently?"
- **0208** Con el tiempo — "How do you say something changed over time?"
- **0209** Con toda probabilidad — "How do you hedge a confident prediction?"
- **0210** Con todo respeto — "How do you disagree politely?"
- **0211** Con atención — "How do you say you're listening closely?"
- **0212** De algún modo — "How do you say you managed it somehow?"
